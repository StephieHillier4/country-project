Culture

African Tribes

Africa is the most naturally diverse continent with over 3,000 tribes, 2,000 languages, 54 countries and 1.5 billion people.Survival International, founded in 1969, was started to fight alongside Indigenous and Tribal peoples to defend their lives and lands. Many tribes of different families live in same areas of Africa in peace, respect and understanding of their differences.

Languages

Africa has one of the greatest concentrations of linguistic diversity in the world with an estimated 3,000 languages, Nigeria alone having over 500. African languages go as far back as cave dwellings with pictorial forms of communication.

Music, Dance and Food

Africa has is an incredibly social economy. Instrumental music techniques, the most common being the Djembe Drum, native dances and incredibly savory dishes, such as Jollof Rice with its rich, spicy tomato flavor, keeps the African people, both young and old, in high spirits and connected to their history and ancestors.
