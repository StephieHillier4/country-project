Culture

African Tribes

Africa is the most naturally diverse continent with over 3,000 tribes, 2,000 languages, 54 countries and 1.5 billion people.Survival International, founded in 1969, was started to fight alongside Indigenous and Tribal peoples to defend their lives and lands.

Languages

Africa has one of the greatest concentrations of linguistic diversity in the world with an estimated 3,000 languages, Nigeria alone having over 500.

Music, Dance and Food

Africa has is an incredibly social economy. Instrumental music techniques, native dances and incredibly savory dishes keeps the African people in high spirits and keeps them connected to their history and ancestors.
