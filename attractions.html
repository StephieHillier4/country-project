Attractions

Cities

Africa has some of the most beautiful cities in world. Capetown, located in South Africa with unforgettable beaches, Cairo, Egypt's Capitol, home to the great pyramids and Stone Town, located in beautiful Zanzibar, known as "Spice Island" for it's abundant flavors.

Excursions

Africa is an incredible continent for adventurers. Kilimanjaro, just over 18,000ft, attracts worldwide hikers, hot air ballooning has become incredibly popular with African tourists, Madagascar Island is home to some of the rarest animal and plant life on the planet, and safaries give visitors a close up with breathtaking wildlife.

Landscapes

Endless beaches, unending mountain jungles, hidden waterfalls and vast deserts, Africa has some the most beautiful landscapes carved out by mother nature over centuries
